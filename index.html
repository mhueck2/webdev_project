About

My name is Max and I am currently a Master's student in Computer Science at Portland State University, graduating 2023. I have a BA in Environmental Science and have previously worked on geophysical exploration surveys around the world.

My academic interests include merging geography with software and machine learning. I want to leverage ML for environmental hazard mitigation, especially fires and floods. I have experience with C++, C, Java, Python, Javascript, HTML, CSS, SQL, and Linux.
